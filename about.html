  • Who Am I?

    I have been on my design journey since I got my hands on Adobe Illustrator at the age of 14. I have since then explored my way through design in high school to Emily Carr, to BCIT and to the IDEA School of Design at Capilano University. Along with my educational journey, I became a leader, helping others to learn and connect during their own design explorations. It is my goal in life to help others creatively through direction, strategy, and creation. I am always up for a challenge and ready for the next adventure. I am a leader, a collaborator and most of all a creative innovator.
