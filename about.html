  • I am Imogen and I am a passionate designer entering the creative field with an interests in all things branding including strategy, packaging, and advertising. By the spring of 2024, I will be an alumnus of Capilano University, Graduating from the IDEA Program.

    I have been on my design journey ever since I got my hands on Adobe Illustrator for the first time when I was only 14. Pursuing my new-found passion, I took any applied arts and design class I could in high school along with going into Emily Carr University's Summer Insitute Program where not only did I excel, but I was my group's representative. In that role, I was able to help plan and design the end-of-summer exhibit with all the student's work on display.

    After I graduated from high school, I set foot into a joint program at both BCIT and Emily Carr University. From that intensive program, I learned to efficiently use Adobe tools but more importantly, it is where I discovered the world of branding and I've never looked back!

    When I was accepted into the IDEA Program, I was ecstatic to start the next chapter in my career path moving the creative industry within reach. Now it is time for me to set out to make my design ambitions come into view.
