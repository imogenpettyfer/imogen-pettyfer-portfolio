While exploring the meaning of my own name, I discovered that Imogen was a strongly spirited character created by Shakespeare in the play, Cymbeline. This knowledge inspired the creation of a fictional brand of gin that distills Imogen’s character in every bottle. It invites time for you to be true to your own spirit.
