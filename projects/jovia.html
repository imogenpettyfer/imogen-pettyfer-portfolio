  • About

Jovia is a cleaning supply company that minimizes waste with reusable coloured glass and stainless-steel bottles. Jovia seeks to make homes shine, and brighten the cleaning experience with chemical-free products that come in dissolvable tablets for refilling reusable bottles.

  • Problem

    Within our communities, we are opting for more sustainable solutions. in the cleaning product world there have been changes to refill depots and dissolvable sheets and tablets, but what about the containers that hold the cleaning solvents? Not to mention how plastic looks on the counter. Not exactly the most appealing.

  • Solution

    Jovia seeks to engage their consumers with combining aesthetics and sustainability in a cleaning product. Additionally, Jovia is focused on proving the sustainability with a one-year warranty on all their glass products. This will strengthen their unique selling point and help effectively convey its brand values and advantages with their products.
