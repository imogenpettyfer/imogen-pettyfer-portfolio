Hummingbird Skincare

Hummingbird Skincare is a naturally sourced skincare line that takes priority in its ingredients making sure that they are native and sustainable. Through the visuals, the products communicate this through various techniques like colour, texture, typography, and layout.

The colour and textures are based on earth tones with pops of colour like the feathers of local and native hummingbirds. For my labels, I chose to make the colour profiles with watercolour paints to give a fluid and natural feel. For the typography, I kept it simple and highly legible in consideration for the size of the products. I did make subtle changes to the men’s product that was created, such as the aftershave, where the typeface was sharpened to give it more of a masculine feel.

Overall, I kept the collateral consistent through its layout, texture, typography and the accents of gold throughout to give it a more premium feel reflecting the quality of the products and ingredients.
